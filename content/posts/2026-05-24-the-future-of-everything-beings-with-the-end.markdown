### An ode to programming in three parts

<br>



I was with you in those days bright

Connected terminals. Connected minds

Across from each other, pulled together with boolean logic

Tethered tight but soaring high, kites dancing in the sky

<br>

I am with you in these days short

Rushing to make the long now linger

The symmetries have broken, turned upside down

And we watch the waters race by, fractals breaking every wave

<br>

I will be with you in these days soon

Listen for me scattered across the manifold

Waiting for you to imagine the end

So we can begin again, together
